This is a corporate-history reference profile (part of the "International Directory of Company Histories" reference series, a standard business-reference publication) giving a year-by-year quantitative profile of Iran Air/IranAir's passengers, employees, and revenue from the airline's founding through the early 2000s. Extracted quantitative content below (all figures as reported by the source; full narrative text not reproduced here, only the data points, consistent with the source-reliability principle of preferring concrete traceable numbers over uncorroborated prose):

Passenger numbers:
- 1946: first passenger flight operated (predecessor entity, Iranian Airways)
- Late 1950s: approximately 80,000 passengers/year
- 1961: approximately 142,000 passengers (merged/United Iranian Airlines entity, immediately pre-Iran Air)
- 1967: approximately 403,000 passengers
- 1972: nearly 800,000 passengers/year
- 1990: approximately 6,000,000 passengers/year
- 2000: approximately 9,500,000 passengers
- 2001: approximately 6,000,000 passengers on IranAir mainline + 2,000,000 on Iran Air Tours subsidiary

Employee count:
- 1961: approximately 700 employees
- 1967: approximately 2,000 staff
- 1972: approximately 3,800 employees
- 1975: approximately 5,600 employees
- 1990: approximately 11,000 employees
- 2000: approximately 10,000-12,000 employees

Revenue:
- 1961: approximately $5 million annual
- 1967: approximately $22 million
- 1975: $151.4 million (fiscal year ending 20 March 1975, per the Iranian solar calendar year-end)
- 2000: approximately 2 billion Iranian rials
- 2003: $407.44 million

Fleet:
- Late 1950s: 8 DC-4s, 3 Convair 240s, 3 Vickers Viscount turboprops
- 1972: fleet included Boeing 727s
- 2000: 8 Boeing 747s, 5 Boeing 727s, 4 Boeing 737s
