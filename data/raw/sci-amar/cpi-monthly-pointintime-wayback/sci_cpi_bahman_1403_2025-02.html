Consumer Price Index for the Month of Bahman, the Year 1403 (Base year 1400=100)

Consumer Price Index for the Month of Bahman[1], the Year 14032 (Base year 1400=100)

Price Index

In the month of Bahman, the year 1403, the national Consumer Price Index (CPI) for households was 305.5, showing a 4.1 percent increase from the previous month. It experienced a growth of 35.3 percent compared to the same month of the previous year, and a rise of 32.0 percent in the 12 months ending the month of Bahman, the year 1403 compared to the last corresponding period.

National Household Point-to-point Inflation Rate

The point-to-point inflation rate is defined as the percentage change in the price index compared to the corresponding month of the previous year. In the month of Bahman of the year 1403, the household point-to-point inflation rate was 35.3 percent. This indicates that national households spent, on average, 35.3 percent more on purchasing the "same goods and services" compared to the month of Bahman in the year 1402. Furthermore, the point-to-point inflation rate of the month of Bahman increased by 3.5 percentage points compared to the previous month (Dey, the year 1403).
