## Metadata (as shown on page)

- **Indicator:** Registered Motor Vehicles
- **Coverage:** 2005 – 2020 (page title says "2005 – 2025" but the visible series/observations only
  go through Dec 2020, with 12 yearly observations)
- **Unit:** Unit th (thousand units)
- **Frequency:** Yearly
- **Original source (per CEIC attribution):** International Organization of Motor Vehicle
  Manufacturers (OICA)
- **CEIC categorization:** World Trend Plus > Association: Automobile Sector > Table
  RA.OICA.VIU: Vehicle In Use: by Country (Discontinued)
- **Status:** CEIC marks this series "active status" in their platform, but the underlying OICA
  table is labeled "(Discontinued)"

## Caveats

- Only 12 yearly observations are described in the free-preview summary text (2005–2020); the
  full year-by-year series, the interactive chart data, and any 2021–2025 updates implied by the
  page title are behind CEIC's paywall and were not accessible.
- "Registered Motor Vehicles" (OICA vehicles-in-use concept) is a different concept from
  "household car ownership rate" — cross-reference against the Iran Open Data /
  Statistical Centre of Iran household-ownership figures in this same source folder
  (`iran-car-ownership/iran-open-data-car-ownership-article/`) rather than treating the two as
  directly comparable.
